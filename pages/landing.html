🌍 Welcome to Mini Minecraft

Explore the world, collect resources, and build your own landscape. Choose the correct tool to break each block and add it to your inventory.

🛠️ Tools

🪓 Axe

Use the Axe to break Trees. After breaking a tree, it is added to your inventory.

⛏️ Pickaxe

Use the Pickaxe to break Stone blocks.

🥄 Shovel

Use the Shovel to dig Dirt and Grass blocks.

🧱 Build

Use the Build tool to place blocks back into the world. First select a block from your Inventory, then choose the Build tool, and click on an empty space to place it. You need at least one of that block in your inventory to build with it.

🎮 How to Play

  1. Select a tool from the toolbar.
  2. Click on a matching block to collect it.
  3. The block is added to your inventory.
  4. Select a block from the inventory to place it back into the world.
  5. Use the correct tool for each block.
